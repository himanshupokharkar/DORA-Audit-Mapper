**Legal basis:** DORA Regulation (EU) 2022/2554, Articles 15 (fourth subparagraph) and 16(3) (fourth subparagraph)
**Scope:** Applies to all financial entities subject to DORA (Title II: full framework) and to financial entities listed in Article 16(1) of DORA (Title III: simplified framework)

**Structure:**
- Title I (Art 1): General proportionality principle
- Title II (Arts 2–27): Full ICT risk management framework — ICT security policies, asset management, encryption, operations security, network security, project/change management, physical security, human resources, access control, incident detection/response, business continuity, and reporting
- Title III (Arts 28–41): Simplified ICT risk management framework for smaller/lower-complexity entities
- Title IV (Art 42): Entry into force
